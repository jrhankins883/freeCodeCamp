Garlic Butter Chicken with Lemon Asparagus & Herb Rice

This is the recipe for the Garlic Butter Chicken with Lemon Asparagus & Herb Rice dish! It has a rich garlic butter sauce, fresh lemon zest, and fragrant herb-seasoned rice to bring everything together.

Ingredients(Click to Reveal) ▼

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

For the Rice:

  • 1 cup jasmine or basmati rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth (for extra flavor)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp dried thyme (or parsley)

For the Asparagus:

  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of ½ lemon
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions(Click to Reveal) ▼

Cook the Rice

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, bring chicken broth, butter, garlic powder, salt, and thyme to a boil.
  3. Stir in the rice, cover, reduce heat to low, and cook for 12-15 minutes. Remove from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

Cook the Chicken:

  1. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown.
  3. Reduce heat to medium, add butter, garlic, lemon juice, and Italian seasoning. Spoon the butter over the chicken and cook for another 2-3 minutes until done (internal temp: 165°F). Set aside.

Saute the Asparagus:

  1. In the same pan, add olive oil and butter over medium heat.
  2. Toss in the asparagus, garlic, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes until tender but still crisp.

Assemble and Serve:

  1. Plate the herb rice, slice the chicken, and place it on top.
  2. Add the asparagus on the side and drizzle any leftover garlic butter sauce from the pan over everything.
