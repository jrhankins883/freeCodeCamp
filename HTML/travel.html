Discover Banff & Lake Louise in Alberta, Canada

Banff National Park, located in Alberta, Canada, is famous for its vibrant lakes, majestic mountains and easy access to outdoor adventures of all kinds. The park spans 6,641 square kilometres (2,564 square miles) of striking mountainous terrain in the vast wilderness of the Canadian Rockies. Canada's first national park and the world's third, it has a rich heritage as one of the world's most awe-inspiring mountain destinations. Ski, bike, hike and so much more, no matter your preference, there's something for everyone.
